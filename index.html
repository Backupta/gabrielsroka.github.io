Show SSO bookmarklet

This bookmarklet shows SAML/WS-Fed assertions and other SSO information.

Setup
1. Show your bookmarks toolbar. In Chrome, ... > Bookmarks > Show Bookmarks Bar. In Firefox/IE, right-click in the title bar and click Bookmarks Toolbar.
2. Drag this to the bookmark toolbar:
Show SSO

Usage
1. Login to Okta.
2. Click the bookmark from your toolbar.
3. Below each chicklet, there's a Show SSO link. Click one to see the SSO information.

Export Groups bookmarklet

This bookmarklet exports groups.

Setup
1. Show your bookmarks toolbar. In Chrome, ... > Bookmarks > Show Bookmarks Bar. In Firefox/IE, right-click in the title bar and click Bookmarks Toolbar.
2. Drag this to the bookmark toolbar:
Export Groups

Usage
1. Using Chrome, login to Okta Admin. Chrome allows you to export 1,000s of groups, whereas Firefox and IE stop at 1,000.
2. Open the dev console (F12).
3. Click the bookmark from your toolbar.
4. When the export is done, the groups are in the console. Save the results to a CSV file.

Export App Users bookmarklet

This bookmarklet exports app users.

Setup
1. Show your bookmarks toolbar. In Chrome, ... > Bookmarks > Show Bookmarks Bar. In Firefox/IE, right-click in the title bar and click Bookmarks Toolbar.
2. Drag this to the bookmark toolbar:
Export App Users

Usage
1. Using Chrome, login to Okta Admin. Chrome allows you to export 1,000s of users, whereas Firefox and IE stop at 1,000.
2. Go to Applications > Applications.
3. Click on an app.
4. Open the dev console (F12).
5. Click the bookmark from your toolbar.
6. When the export is done, the users are in the console. Save the results to a CSV file.
