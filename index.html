Show SSO bookmarklet

This bookmarklet shows SAML/WS-Fed assertions and other SSO information.

Setup
1. Show your bookmarks toolbar. In Chrome, ... > Bookmarks > Show Bookmarks Bar. In Firefox/IE, right-click in the title bar and click Bookmarks Toolbar.
2. Drag this to the bookmark toolbar:
Show SSO

Usage
1. Login to Okta.
2. Click the bookmark from your toolbar.
3. Below each chicklet, there's a Show SSO link. Click one to see the SSO information.

Export Objects bookmarklet

This bookmarklet exports objects, such as Users, Groups, Directory Users, App Users, App Groups.

Setup
1. Show your bookmarks toolbar. In Chrome, ... > Bookmarks > Show Bookmarks Bar. In Firefox/IE, right-click in the title bar and click Bookmarks Toolbar.
2. Drag this to the bookmark toolbar:
Export Objects

Usage
1. Using Chrome, login to Okta Admin. Chrome allows you to export 1,000s of objects, whereas Firefox and IE stop at 1,000.
2. Go to one of these:
  • Directory > People
  • Directory > Groups
  • Directory > Directory Integrations and click on a directory
  • Applications > Applications and click on an app
3. Open the dev console (F12).
4. Click the bookmark from your toolbar.
5. When the export is done, the objects are in the console. Save the results to a CSV file.
